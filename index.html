Hello, Welcome to PADAYON!
PADAYON!' is our way of saying we're all about student life, study tips, self-improvement, and other educational stuff. So, if you hear PADAYON!, think of it as your go-to for learning and growth, both mentally and physically. Prepare for our short adventure as we share our knowledge with other students and explore the exciting world of PADAYON!
What is PADAYON?
Padayon is a term meaning 'to continue,' that embodies our educational content. This message reminds everyone, especially students, to continue working hard. Regardless of the challenges, let this serve as a source of motivation, encouraging all to remain dedicated and persevere in the pursuit of their goals. In the Visayan Language, "Padayon" means to "carry on," and this reflects our commitment to supporting students in overcoming certain problems, good or worse, we focus on giving education as a top priority. So, if you hear PADAYON!, think of it as your go-to for learning and growth, both mentally and physically.
